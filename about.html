About Us

We are a full-service event planning firm based in Bengaluru, India, run by our Founder & Director, Shweta G Malliik. We are highly motivated individuals who are creative, innovative, and extremely professional. Our events are well thought out and curated to our clients’ specifications for optimal customer satisfaction.

Our team always strives hard to provide the experience, expertise, and bespoke services that result in sheer perfection coupled with the right proportion of elegance, style, and sophistication. We guarantee you impeccable event planning and execution services with a 100% success rate, stress-free engagement, and an event that is absolutely flawless. We look forward to giving complete attention to our client’s event in order to make it one of the most spectacular and memorable events for our clients.

With years of experience in the event planning industry, the team Celebrators stands on a stronger foundation having the most creative, enthusiastic, and passionate team members on board who have developed their expertise in the event planning industry.
